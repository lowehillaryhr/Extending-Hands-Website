Our Mission

Faith-guided compassion that leads to practical change.

Extending Hands supports individuals seeking stability by providing faith-guided compassion, resources, and encouragement that help them move from hardship toward renewed hope.

Our Vision

Resilient communities where people overcome hardship.

Extending Hands envisions resilient communities where individuals overcome hardship and achieve stability through faith-based support, essential resources, and sustainable opportunities.

Who We Serve

Extending Hands proudly serves individuals and families throughout Middle Georgia who are working toward greater stability, opportunity, and well-being.

  • Single mothers and pregnant women seeking support and stability
  • Low-income households navigating food and resource needs
  • Seniors who benefit from outreach, check-ins, and connection
  • Job seekers and career builders preparing for employment
  • Individuals facing transitions who need encouragement and guidance
  • Any community member seeking stability, support, or a new beginning
